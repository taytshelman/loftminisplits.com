Questions

What everyone asks first.

How much does a mini split cost installed?
A single-room 12,000 BTU system starts at $985 installed. Bigger rooms and multi-zone systems cost more because the equipment is bigger, but you will know your exact number before we schedule anything. Quotes are free.
Do mini splits actually work in a Kansas winter?
Yes. Modern cold-climate units heat efficiently well below freezing, and the same unit cools all summer. That is the whole point: one system, both extremes.
How long does installation take?
About 1.5 to 2 hours for a single zone. Larger 220v systems usually take around 2 hours. Most jobs are finished the same morning we start.
What is the warranty?
5-year manufacturer parts warranty, plus our own 2-year labor warranty on the install itself.
Do you work with rentals and multi-unit properties?
Yes. If you are an investor putting units in multiple properties or rooms on the same job, ask about multi-unit pricing.
